About Me

I am an electrical engineer currently pursuing a master's degree with a focus on AI and computer vision. With a robust foundation in computer science and VLSI from my bachelor's degree, I have also gained practical experience in the memory chips industry. My passion for tackling complex problems is matched by my enthusiasm for art; I spend much of my free time painting. I am approachable and enjoy collaborating with others, always eager to learn and explore new ideas, continually seeking to blend technical expertise with creative expression. If you’re interested in artistic collaborations or have a project in mind that blends technology and creativity, I’d love to connect and explore how we can work together.
